Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Trace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Trace, we collect the following personal information:

  • Google Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (obtained via Google OAuth)
  • Account Data: User ID, subscription status, and authentication tokens
  • Payment Information: Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status, not credit card details

Usage Information

  • Page Content: Text content from web pages you explicitly choose to summarize
  • Reading Preferences: Your selected reading level, reader type, and summary style preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Number of API requests, timestamps, and feature usage data
  • Feedback: Any feedback you voluntarily submit through our feedback feature

Technical Information

  • Browser Information: Chrome extension version, browser type
  • Log Data: API endpoints accessed, error logs, and timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide AI-powered summarization of web content based on your preferences
  • Personalization: To customize summaries according to your reading level and style
  • Authentication: To securely identify and authenticate users
  • Subscription Management: To process payments and manage your subscription status
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your feedback and support requests
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered content summarization. Page content you submit is processed by OpenAI's API. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Cloud Hosting: We use secure cloud hosting infrastructure to store and process your data

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication: JWT tokens for secure API authentication
  • Database Security: User data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With third-party services mentioned above that help us operate our service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition (users will be notified)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection
  • Subscription Cancellation: Cancel your subscription through the extension settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Trace is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

9. International Users

Trace is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notice through the extension (for material changes)

Your continued use of Trace after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Cookie Policy

Trace uses browser storage (localStorage and chrome.storage) to:

  • Store authentication tokens
  • Save your reading preferences
  • Cache subscription status
  • Maintain session state

We do not use third-party cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
